Camp Worden


Camp Worden is what my family calls my grandma's house. My grandma's five kids each take turns bringing their families to her house each summer to enjoy the beach and eat fish and chips. Every year, someone designs a unique shirt for that year and distributes one to each grandchild (I think there are 15) and one to grandma. The symbol of Camp Worden is the flagpole that sits on my grandma's front lawn, looking out over Buzzard's Bay.
