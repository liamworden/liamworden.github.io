Mad Libs

Per Wikipedia: Mad Libs is a phrasal template word game created by Leonard Stern and Roger Price. It consists of one player prompting others for a list of words to substitute for blanks in a story before reading aloud. The game is frequently played as a party game or as a pastime.

Basically, I'm going to ask you to fill in a couple blanks. After that, you'll be given the story you helped write!
